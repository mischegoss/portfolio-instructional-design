The Design Challenge

The real barrier to automation adoption isn't technical knowledge. It's organizational confidence. Every design decision here addresses that specific problem.

About This Module

This module is the entry point to Resolve's automation learning program, designed to help IT teams move from thinking about automation to concrete first steps.

Design Decisions
  • The Automatable persona provides a concrete real-world example throughout each module, allowing the learner to see the progress from concept to implementation.
  • The start-small framework (Choose → Define Success → Measure → Celebrate) is deliberately low-stakes to prevent overwhelming learners. The goal is a single successful automation to build credibility and provide a replicable model for success.
  • Measurable outcomes are baked in from step one. This gives learners a concrete completion condition for the activity and a ready-made framework for communicating impact upward.
  • The interactive form guides users step-by-step. It can also be exported as a PDF to share with other decision-makers, so the learning transfers directly into a real workflow conversation.
  • RANI, the embedded AI assistant, is positioned inside the module itself to allow direct answers to learners' questions without leaving the learning space.
